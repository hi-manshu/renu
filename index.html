Our story

Renu Bardia, a mother who was always compassionate about creating new things discovered her love for cakes while baking for her kids.On her child’s birthday she always used to come up with ideas each unique in itself. She always loved to add that extra edge to the regular cakes too. She envisaged her journey of baking in 2016 and at the age of 49 she still holds the passion to try out this venture and make a stand in this world. Her path on baking started with baking for her kids to now offering a range of delicious cakes, chocolates and muffins to her customers. Her love for baking brings out that special aroma in her cakes and she truly proves that baking is love made edible.
